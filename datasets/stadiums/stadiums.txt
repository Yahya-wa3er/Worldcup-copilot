===============================================================================
OFFICIAL STADIUMS OF THE FIFA WORLD CUP 2026 (USA, MEXICO, CANADA)
===============================================================================

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. UNITED STATES (11 Stadiums)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Stadium: MetLife Stadium
City: East Rutherford, New Jersey (New York/New Jersey)
Country: USA
Capacity: 82500
Notes: Host of the 2026 World Cup Final. Located near New York City.
Transport: NJ Transit train from Penn Station, 30 minutes.
Nearby hotels: Marriott Meadowlands, Hilton Hasbrouck Heights.

Stadium: AT&T Stadium
City: Arlington, Texas (Dallas)
Country: USA
Capacity: 80000
Notes: Home of the Dallas Cowboys. Features a massive center-hung video board. Host of a semi-final.
Transport: Trinity Railway Express from Dallas, 20 minutes.
Nearby hotels: Sheraton Arlington, Hilton Arlington.

Stadium: SoFi Stadium
City: Inglewood, California (Los Angeles)
Country: USA
Capacity: 70240
Notes: Modern facility opened in 2020. Host of the US Men's National Team opening match.
Transport: Metro K Line from downtown LA, 40 minutes.
Nearby hotels: The Westin Los Angeles Airport, Hilton Los Angeles.

Stadium: Mercedes-Benz Stadium
City: Atlanta, Georgia
Country: USA
Capacity: 71000
Notes: Retractable roof stadium, home of Atlanta United (MLS). Host of a semi-final.
Transport: MARTA rail system (GWCC/CNN Center Station), direct access.
Nearby hotels: Omni Atlanta Hotel at Centennial Park, Glenn Hotel.

Stadium: Gillette Stadium
City: Foxborough, Massachusetts (Boston)
Country: USA
Capacity: 65878
Notes: Home of the New England Patriots. Located southwest of Boston.
Transport: MBTA Commuter Rail from Boston South Station (Special event service).
Nearby hotels: Hilton Garden Inn Foxborough, Renaissance Boston Patriot Place.

Stadium: Hard Rock Stadium
City: Miami Gardens, Florida (Miami)
Country: USA
Capacity: 64767
Notes: Host of the 2024 Copa America Final. Features an open-air canopy. Host of the bronze medal match.
Transport: Brightline Train to Aventura Station + shuttle, or taxi/rideshare.
Nearby hotels: Stadium Hotel, JW Marriott Turnberry Resort.

Stadium: NRG Stadium
City: Houston, Texas
Country: USA
Capacity: 72220
Notes: Retractable roof stadium, home of the Houston Texans.
Transport: METRORail Red Line from Downtown Houston, 20 minutes.
Nearby hotels: Marriott Marquis Houston, Hilton Americas-Houston.

Stadium: Arrowhead Stadium (GEHA Field)
City: Kansas City, Missouri
Country: USA
Capacity: 76416
Notes: Known for its historic atmosphere and breaking noise level world records.
Transport: RideKC Bus routes from downtown, or designated rideshare zones.
Nearby hotels: InterContinental Kansas City at the Plaza, The Fontaine.

Stadium: Lincoln Financial Field
City: Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
Country: USA
Capacity: 69796
Notes: Home of the Philadelphia Eagles. Located in South Philadelphia Sport Complex.
Transport: SEPTA Broad Street Line (NRG Station), 15 minutes from Center City.
Nearby hotels: Live! Casino & Hotel Philadelphia, Courtyard Philadelphia South.

Stadium: Levi's Stadium
City: Santa Clara, California (San Francisco Bay Area)
Country: USA
Capacity: 68500
Notes: Highly eco-friendly stadium located in Silicon Valley.
Transport: VTA Light Rail directly to Great America Station, or Caltrain connection.
Nearby hotels: Hyatt Regency Santa Clara, Hilton Santa Clara.

Stadium: Lumen Field
City: Seattle, Washington
Country: USA
Capacity: 69000
Notes: Home of Seattle Sounders (MLS), famous for its steep seating and loud acoustics.
Transport: Link Light Rail to Stadium or International District Station, 5 minutes.
Nearby hotels: Silver Cloud Hotel Seattle Stadium, Embassy Suites Pioneer Square.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. MEXICO (3 Stadiums)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Stadium: Estadio Azteca
City: Mexico City
Country: Mexico
Capacity: 87523
Notes: Most iconic stadium in the World Cup 2026. Host of the tournament's opening match. First stadium to host three World Cups.
Transport: Xochimilco Light Rail (Tren Ligero) from Tasqueña Metro Station.
Nearby hotels: Camino Real Pedregal, Radisson Paraiso Mexico City.

Stadium: Estadio BBVA (Estadio Monterrey)
City: Guadalupe, Nuevo León (Monterrey)
Country: Mexico
Capacity: 53500
Notes: Nicknamed "El Gigante de Acero" (The Steel Giant), famous for its mountain views.
Transport: Monterrey Metro Line 1 to Exposición Station + short walk.
Nearby hotels: Quinta Real Monterrey, Live Aqua Urban Resort Monterrey.

Stadium: Estadio Akron (Estadio Guadalajara)
City: Zapopan, Jalisco (Guadalajara)
Country: Mexico
Capacity: 48071
Notes: Distinctive grass-covered hill exterior shape, home of Chivas.
Transport: Macrobus/Mi Transporte bus lines, or dedicated event shuttles.
Nearby hotels: Hyatt Regency Andares Guadalajara, Hilton Guadalajara Midtown.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. CANADA (2 Stadiums)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Stadium: BC Place
City: Vancouver, British Columbia
Country: Canada
Capacity: 54500
Notes: Located in downtown Vancouver. Features the world's largest cable-supported retractable roof.
Transport: SkyTrain (Expo Line to Stadium-Chinatown Station), 5 minutes.
Nearby hotels: Westin Grand Vancouver, Sheraton Vancouver Wall Centre.

Stadium: BMO Field (Toronto Stadium)
City: Toronto, Ontario
Country: Canada
Capacity: 45736
Notes: Canada's national soccer stadium, home of Toronto FC. Expanded for the World Cup.
Transport: GO Transit train to Exhibition Station, or TTC Streetcar (509/511).
Nearby hotels: Hotel X Toronto, Gladstone House.
