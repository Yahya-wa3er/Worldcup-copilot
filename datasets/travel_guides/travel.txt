# FIFA WORLD CUP 2026 — GLOBAL TRAVEL OPERATIONS GUIDE

########################################
## OVERVIEW
########################################

The 2026 World Cup is unique due to:
- 3 host countries
- continental scale travel
- complex visa systems
- high tourism pressure

---

########################################
## VISA SYSTEMS
########################################

### UNITED STATES
- ESTA (visa waiver countries)
- B1/B2 tourist visa required for others
- strict border control procedures

---

### CANADA
- eTA (electronic authorization)
- visa depending on nationality

---

### MEXICO
- simplified tourist visa policy
- high accessibility for global fans

---

########################################
## TRANSPORT INFRASTRUCTURE
########################################

### AIR TRAVEL (MAIN SYSTEM)
- USA has largest domestic aviation network in world
- Major hubs:
  - New York (JFK)
  - Dallas (DFW)
  - Los Angeles (LAX)
  - Miami (MIA)
  - Toronto (YYZ)
  - Mexico City (MEX)

---

### DOMESTIC TRAVEL CHALLENGE
Key issue:
- distances between cities can exceed 3,000 km

Example:
- New York → Los Angeles: ~5,000 km

---

### RAIL SYSTEM
- Limited high-speed rail in USA
- Canada rail usable but slower
- Mexico intercity rail improving but limited coverage

---

########################################
## CLIMATE CONDITIONS
########################################

### USA
- varies by region:
  - East Coast: humid + warm
  - West Coast: mild
  - South: very hot

### Mexico
- hot + altitude variations (Mexico City important factor)

### Canada
- mild summer temperatures (ideal for football)

---

########################################
## FAN EXPERIENCE STRATEGY
########################################

Recommendations:
- book hotels 6–12 months in advance
- avoid last-minute flights (price surge expected)
- prioritize city clusters instead of cross-country travel
- use FIFA fan zones for cheaper experience

---

########################################
## COST STRUCTURE (ESTIMATED)

- Flights: $600–1500 international
- Hotels: $120–400/night
- Match tickets: $50–$1000+
- Local transport: $20–$200 per city

---

########################################
## STRATEGIC INSIGHT
########################################

This World Cup is:
- logistically the most complex in history
- financially the most expensive for fans
- but also the most accessible in terms of number of teams and matches
